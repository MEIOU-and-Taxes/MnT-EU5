# Rosth: edited minimum and maximum number of privileges for each estate

# Nobles		Nobles are the hardest estate to strike a balance between loyalty, power and taxation with. Since some of the privileges for other estate also piss nobles off, I'll keep the maximum number of allowed privileges high, but counter it with the lower minimum.
# Clergy		Mostly good privileges and keeping clergy happy gives a research speed bonus. Safe to give them a decent number of privileges.
# Burghers		Almost all privileges are good and they usually stay relatively weak. Safe to go crazy with privileges to keep them happy and taxable.
# Peasants		Decent estate with overall decent privileges. I wouldn't really give them too many of them though.
# Dhimmi		Decent estate when it comes to taxation, although the privileges are mediocre at best since they often come with penalties to noble and clergy satisfaction. Giving them a couple of privileges should be fine.
# Tribes		Meh estate, almost always has no power even in tribal nations, brings no taxes whatsoever. Safe to give them a couple of privileges for their loyalty bonuses but that's about it.
# Cossacks		Decent estate with decent privileges, if a bit rare. Safe to give them a decent number of privileges.
